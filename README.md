# 📊 Consumer Behavior & Shopping Habits Analysis

### Targeted Marketing Strategy using Data Analytics & Power BI

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes consumer purchasing behavior using a retail dataset to uncover patterns in **seasonality, demographics, product preferences, and promotional impact**.

The objective is to transform raw data into **actionable business insights** and build an **interactive Power BI dashboard** to support data-driven marketing decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

* What factors influence customer purchase decisions?
* Which seasons drive higher sales?
* Which customer segments spend more?
* Are promotions (discounts & promo codes) effective?

---

## 📂 Dataset

* **Source:** Kaggle – Consumer Behavior Dataset
* **Records:** 3,900 rows | **Features:** 18 columns
* Includes: Age, Gender, Category, Purchase Amount, Season, Promotions, Payment Method

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Python:** Pandas, NumPy
* **Visualization:** Matplotlib, Seaborn
* **BI Tool:** Power BI
* **Environment:** Jupyter Notebook

## 🔍 Key Insights

* ❄️ Higher spending in **Winter & Autumn**
* 👕 **Clothing** is the most popular category
* 👨 Male customers contribute ~67% of revenue
* 📦 XL size has lowest purchase frequency
* 🎟️ Promo codes show **low impact**
* 🌍 Montana has highest customer concentration
* 👥 Footwear popular among **45–55 age group**

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus campaigns during **peak seasons (Winter/Autumn)**
* Prioritize **Clothing category** for revenue growth
* Target **high-spending customer segments (Male)**
* Re-evaluate **promo/discount strategy**
* Optimize inventory for **high-demand sizes (S, M, L)**
* Invest in **high-performing locations**

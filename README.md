# Consumer-Behavior-Analysis-1
## 📌 Project Overview
This project performs an in-depth analysis of consumer shopping habits and behavior using a dataset focused on retail trends. The goal is to identify patterns related to customer demographics, seasonality, product attributes, and promotional effectiveness to help businesses design more effective **targeted marketing strategies**.

## 🚀 Key Objectives
- **Seasonality Impact:** Analyze how different seasons influence purchase decisions across various product categories.
- **Demographic Insights:** Explore the relationship between age/gender and shopping preferences.
- **Promotional Analysis:** Evaluate the effectiveness of discounts and promo codes on overall sales and customer ratings.
- **Geographic Trends:** Identify regional spending habits and top-performing locations.

## 📊 Key Findings
* **Seasonality:** Customers exhibit higher purchase volumes during **Winter and Autumn** compared to Spring and Summer.
* **Gender Distribution:** The analysis shows a significant difference in spending, with **Males accounting for 67%** of purchases compared to **32% for Females**.
* **Size Preferences:** Standard sizes (Small, Medium, Large) dominate sales, while **XL** shows notably lower purchase frequency.
* **Promo Codes:** Surprisingly, the data suggests that **Promo Codes had no significant impact** on the final purchase decision or amount in this specific dataset.
* **Top Locations:** **Montana** leads in terms of total customer count, while **Alaska** records the highest average spending in the Clothing category ($67).
